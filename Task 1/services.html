Our Services

At Chutku, we offer a range of services designed to cater to the needs of both cats and their owners. Our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional care and support to ensure the health, happiness, and well-being of your feline companions.
1. Veterinary Care:
Our experienced veterinarians provide comprehensive medical care for cats of all ages. From routine check-ups and vaccinations to emergency care and surgery, we're here to keep your cat healthy and thriving.
2. Grooming Services:
Treat your cat to a pampering session with our professional grooming services. From baths and brush-outs to nail trims and ear cleaning, we'll help your cat look and feel their best.
3. Boarding and Daycare:
When you're away, trust us to provide a safe and comfortable home away from home for your cat. Our boarding and daycare services offer personalized care and attention in a clean and secure environment.
